Courtney

Coach & Facilitator, Signal Institute

Courtney Smith is a coach and group facilitator who works with Fortune 500 companies and high-profile individuals to achieve change. She is a renowned practitioner of the Enneagram personality system, which she teaches alongside other methods in her integrated approach to personal development.

Courtney is the co-author, with Elise Loehnen, of Choosing Wholeness Over Goodness (Penguin Random House). Her work sits at the intersection of deep personal inquiry and practical organizational change.

Having lived in New York City for twenty years, Courtney now resides in Santa Barbara, California with her family.
